About Us

Your Travel Comrade

Welcome to YatraMitra, your ultimate travel companion. YatraMitra was created for the WebKriti competition by a team of three dedicated members. Our platform offers a seamless and comprehensive solution for booking airline, train, and bus tickets. With YatraMitra, you can explore various travel options, compare prices, and plan your trips with ease.

We aim to provide the best travel experience by ensuring a user-friendly interface, reliable services, and up-to-date information. Whether you're planning a vacation, a business trip, or just a weekend getaway, YatraMitra is here to assist you every step of the way.
